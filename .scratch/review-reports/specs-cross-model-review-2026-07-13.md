### R.10 [HIGH] — Architecture soundness
**File**: `specs/02-requirements.md:46-47` (REQ-041, CDC events) + `specs/04-architecture.md` §7B.4-§7B.6
**Issue**: There is an unguarded TOCTOU window between Layer 2 authorization completion and response delivery. For V2: consider a post-retrieval, pre-response CDC re-check for `retention_expired` on cited doc_ids, or a response-delivery timestamp compared against the last known CDC event timestamp.
**Status**: ✅ Fixed 2026-07-13 — verified this is a real, previously-uncovered gap distinct from both DEC-091's KV-cache fix (covers *later-turn* resurfacing, not this turn's in-flight response) and `01-product-brief.md`'s existing RISK-017 (covers CDC delivery lag, not the always-present single-check-then-no-recheck sequencing gap that exists even with zero lag). ### R.30 [LOW] — Architecture soundness
